Bio

I am a tech generalist, with an interest in building solutions to socio-economic problems (towards the SDGs 2030 of UN). I graduated from the computer science department of IIT Kanpur in 2016 and have built a diverse skillset through my internships and work experiences at various companies, ranging from the likes of Microsoft Research & Goldman Sachs to tiny startups, with less than 10 people, in education, healthcare and gaming industries.

I am currently building Vibinex for simplifying the process of technical team building, especially in software. Alongside this, I am also thoroughly involved with FOSS United and all their initiatives for promoting Free (as in "Freedom", and not money-wise) and Open-source Software.

I am also working on my own open-source project, Free Time, which is a suite of productivity tools for individuals that helps them do more and live happier.

Towards United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (2030)

I founded Vibinex in 2020 as a mentorship platform. It has taken the shape of a technical hiring platform for software workers. Hence, it is helping us towards both SDG4 (Education) and SDG8 (Economic Growth)
