  • Mind uploading

    Assumptions:

    • Physicalism

      Your mind is in your brain's structure, arrangement and bio-chemistry

    • Scanability

      At some point, we will understand the brain well-enough and possess the technology to simulate all its aspects to make a digital mind copy

    • Computability

      Computer software can host your mind.

      Which means: the mind is computable: There is no physical property in the brain, including consciousness, that cannot be simulated accurately, even it requires a lot of code.

    Beyond these assumptions, we need to understand that our perception of existence is not limited to our consciousness, nor is it limited to our brain. As pointed out in the above video, hormones, heart nerves and the gut bacteria also have an influence on our desires and behaviors.

    • Phase 1: Real Democracy

      This is part of the auto-vote project that I have started. The goal of the project is to eliminate the middle men in our democracies. Currently there are people, who we call "representatives", who are not, even my a long shot, any kind of representatives for ourselves. We want to eliminate their existence. We want to be able to vote on each bill ourselves. But not everyone can devote that much time and energy and not everyone even has the mental faculties to be able to make the decision of what suits them more.

      That is why, it is important for us to upload a part of our minds, the part which is able to encapsulate what affects us. What is that we want and if a particular policy will be helpful for us or not. The output of such a model is pretty simply, a Boolean. But the road to achieving that Boolean output is challenging. Challenging, is a gross understatement.

      To be inspired to solve this problem, imagine that all the 'representatives' do not exist anymore. Which will mean that, we do not have a parliament, implying that there are NO elections of candidates, only election of bills. Which means no political parties, no election debates and no propaganda for political power. We will completely transform the power struggle to something else completely. There will be more room for philosophers among the bureaucrats, as that position would be critical to be able to craft policies, which can be accepted by a over-whelming majority (we will need to define what that means as well).

      As a first step in this project, I have started this FOSS project:

      This project is starting off as something that brings the center of voting to an individuals and his/her own preferences. To make them choose their 'representatives' better. We would like to eventually evolve this project such that it can automatically recommend the best candidate to vote for, based on the preferences of the individual and the history and proposals of the candidate.

    • Phase 2: Mental healthcare

      This is a more inward looking outcome that we are hoping to achieve. Preventions as well as cures for mental illnesses would lead to a more fulfilling life for all individuals. As well as the key to internal-drive can lead to rapid progress of our civilization.

    • Phase 3: The copy or the companion project

      In this phase, we do not have to create a full copy of a self. Just a copy which is advanced enough to have the potential of processing input and information as well as you (the user) do. This can be a solution to many problems like loneliness, anxiety, over-thinking (or under-thinking, as I like to call it), critical brainstorming of unpopular ideas and many more things, that currently require like-minded people to come together and invest a lot in feeling safe together and establishing context to be able to hold a fruitful conversation.

      It should be able to provide critical thinking, with the same biases as the individual, whose copy it is. It must be able to download the life experiences of the individual, and at some level, be able to process all the information to the same capacity.

      The only reason this is not the ultimate product, is that it still lacks sentience and consciousness of the individual.

      This is also why it would be simpler, but also why it cannot provide any of the benefits of immortality or digital existence, as discussed in the above Kurzgesagt video.
